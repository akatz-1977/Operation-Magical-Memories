Operation: Magical Memories — iPhone-Friendly Web App

How to use:
1. Upload these files to any simple web host (GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or your own site).
2. Open the hosted link in Safari on your iPhone.
3. Tap Share.
4. Tap “Add to Home Screen.”
5. The app saves checkboxes, wait times, notes, and live-planner entries on the phone.

Included:
- All seven parks and your selected rides
- Everyone / Some / Waiting labels
- Morning / Midday (A/C) / Evening filters
- Motion-sickness ratings
- Ride completion checkboxes
- Wait-time and notes fields
- Live planner
- Park-bag checklist
- End-of-day checklist
- Photo tracker
